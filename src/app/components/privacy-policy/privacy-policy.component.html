Information Collection And Use

While using our apps, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you. Personally identifiable information is not limited to your name only.

Log Data

Like many apps on the application stores, we also collect information from our users.

The sole purpose of collecting this information is to maintain a log to be used by our own machine learning algorithms. This log data makes it possible for youcan to help its users perform enhanced searching for events.

This Log Data may include information such as timestamp, latitude and longitude, phone type, OS version, the views of our app that you visit, the time and date of your visit, the time spent on those views and other similar statistics.

In addition, we may use third party platforms to collect relevant information from our users like; Facebook Graph API, Google Client API, Google Places API

We deeply care about our users and their privacy, and we do not share their data with any third parties.
